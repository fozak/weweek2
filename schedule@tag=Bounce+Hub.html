I-Corps Final Pitches

  • Friday, November 22, 2019
  • 12:00 PM 2:00 PM 12:00 14:00
  • Bounce Innovation Hub Signal Tree Room (map)
  • Google Calendar ICS

Watch 18 early-stage startup companies give their first pitches! This event caps off the 7-week I-Corps customer discovery program and will feature a series of rapid fire 5-minute pitches. Business ideas typically cross all sectors, including new software concepts, consumer products, deep technologies, medtech and everything in between. Join us for a light lunch, the pitches and an opportunity to network.

Keep in mind - I-Corps will be accepting new applicants in 2020.

Tentative Summer 2020 Dates:

  • Tuesdays and Fridays from June 5-June 2
